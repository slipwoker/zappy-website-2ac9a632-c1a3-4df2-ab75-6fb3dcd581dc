What to expect.

Clear answers to the questions we hear most often. If you need more details, we're here to help.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, fitted activewear that allows you to move freely. Grip socks are recommended but not required.

Do I need experience?

Not at all. We welcome beginners and design each session to meet you exactly where you are.

How do private sessions work?

Private sessions are one-on-one, fully tailored to your goals, schedule, and body. Book through our contact page.

What if I have an injury?

Let us know before your first session. We'll modify movements to support your recovery and keep you safe.

How quickly will I feel results?

Most clients notice improved posture and energy within two weeks. Strength and flexibility build steadily from there.

What equipment is used?

We use reformers, mats, resistance bands, and small props. Everything you need is provided in the studio.
