The studio behind the work.

We train with precision and intention, building strength from the inside out. The atmosphere is focused yet welcoming—a space where movement becomes meditation.

Precision. Care. Transformation.

Founded on the belief that movement should honor the body, Pilates by Lilosh began as a passion project and grew into a sanctuary for those seeking mindful strength training and expert guidance.

A cinematic approach to movement.

Every session is choreographed like a scene—precise cueing, intentional pacing, and a focus on quality over quantity. We don't just count reps; we craft progressions that challenge your body and mind in equal measure.

This is Pilates as a practice of craft, where breath, alignment, and control converge into something transformative. Each movement is an opportunity to refine, to strengthen, to discover what your body is capable of when guided with intention.

Coaching, not just counting reps.

Expect tactile cues that help you feel the work in the right places, breath patterns that anchor each exercise, and alignment adjustments that make every movement more effective. Progress is measurable—not just in strength, but in how you move through your day with greater ease and awareness.

What we protect in every session.

Safety-first alignment

Progress through consistency
